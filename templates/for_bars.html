For Bars

SiplyGo helps venues deliver quicker service, increase order value, and give staff clearer visibility into what guests need next.

Why venues choose SiplyGo

  • Digital menus that stay fresh – highlight specials, mark items out of stock, and share allergen details instantly.
  • Faster fulfilment – bar dashboards show incoming orders, prep status, and ready times in one place.
  • Flexible payments – accept wallet credit, cards, or pay-at-bar while keeping transaction fees predictable.
  • Insights that matter – track nightly revenue, payouts, and repeat guests without spreadsheets.

How onboarding works

  1. We map your menu, categories, and pricing in a shared workspace.
  2. Staff receive a walkthrough covering live orders, notifications, and service best practices.
  3. Go live with marketing assets, QR codes, and support resources tailored to your bar.

What you need

  • A tablet or laptop behind the bar to monitor orders.
  • Reliable Wi-Fi for staff devices and optional guest QR scanning.
  • A point of contact to manage payouts and menu updates.
