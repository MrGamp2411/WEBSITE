Help Center

Find answers to the most common questions about browsing bars, placing orders, and managing your account. These guides apply to both the web app and the mobile experience.

Getting started

  1. Create an account or log in with your existing credentials.
  2. Share your location or search for a city to find nearby venues.
  3. Select a bar, pick your table, and add items to the cart.
  4. Review your order, choose a payment method, and submit.

After placing an order you can track the live status from the Orders page or receive updates via notifications.

Managing your account

  • Update profile details, phone number, and password from the Profile page.
  • Top up credits, review transactions, and download receipts in the Wallet.
  • Check unread messages from bar staff or administrators in Notifications.

Need more help?

Send us a note at support@siplygo.example.com. Include the bar name, order code, and a short description so we can assist quickly.

We respond Monday through Friday, 9:00-18:00 CET.
